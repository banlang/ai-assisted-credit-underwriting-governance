# Disclaimer — AI-Assisted Credit Underwriting Governance Workflow

---

## Scope and Purpose

This repository is a **governance design artifact**. It is intended to demonstrate responsible AI design thinking for credit underwriting decision support in financial services environments.

This artifact is not a deployable banking system.
This artifact is not a credit scoring model.
This artifact is not an automated decisioning engine.
This artifact does not approve, reject, or recommend final credit decisions.

It is a structured design that illustrates how AI and humans can work together in a regulated underwriting environment — with governance controls, transparency, and human accountability at every stage.

---

## Data

No customer-level records from any dataset are included in this repository.

This artifact references the **[Home Credit Default Risk](https://www.kaggle.com/c/home-credit-default-risk/data)** dataset (Kaggle) as a public reference only — to ground the workflow design in realistic applicant data fields. No model is trained on this data. No statistical analysis is performed. No records are extracted or reproduced.

All applicant examples in this repository are entirely synthetic. They do not represent real individuals, real credit applications, or real financial outcomes.

---

## AI and Automation

No automated credit decision is made at any stage of this workflow design.

AI is designed to summarize risk signals only. All final credit decisions remain with human underwriters. The workflow is designed to support human review — not to replace it.

---

## Governance Framework References

References to NIST AI RMF, OWASP LLM Top 10, and IAPP AIGP concepts in this artifact are used as design lenses only.

This artifact does not claim formal compliance with any regulatory framework, certification body, or legal standard. Framework references are included to demonstrate governance-informed design thinking — not to assert regulatory approval or legal adequacy.

---

## Limitations

This artifact reflects a governance design at a point in time. It does not account for institution-specific underwriting policies, jurisdiction-specific regulatory requirements, or operational constraints of any particular financial services organization.

Controls described in this artifact are designed to reduce risk. They are not guarantees of risk elimination.

---

## Intended Use

This artifact is intended for:

- Governance portfolio demonstration
- AI governance design reference
- Professional development and governance capability communication

It is not intended for deployment, regulatory submission, or use as a formal risk management framework.
